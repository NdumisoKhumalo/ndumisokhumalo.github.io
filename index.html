About

More About Me.

I’m a versatile creative, who specializes in Video Production and Multi-media Design. It’s been through a hunger to learn and expand my skills that I’ve had the privilege to work with some of South Africa’s biggest brands, and through a hands-on attitude that I’ve mastered several roles across all phases of the Production Pipeline.

Throughout my career, I have always tried to ensure that the passion and enthusiasm for my work has shone through. Despite my success as an independent designer, I have found that it is through a team that I have achieved my greatest potential. I have learnt that having a platform to question and develop ideas harnesses greater creativity - although realising creative inspiration doesn’t come without its obstacles! Time management and working to tight deadlines are important in any industry, but the foundations of design lay in communication, organization and problem-solving.

In all my work, I have relished these challenges and used them to fuel my ambition and maintain my own high standards.

Services

What I Do.

Part of my role as a Creative Designer is to identify specific problems and create design solutions for my clients. It is through this drive to solve problems that I've mastered the following skills.

  • Branding

    Corporate Branding, Personal Branding, Online & Offline Branding, and Service Branding.

  • Videography

    Corporate Videography: Company Products and Services Promos. Making videos that promote or sell a companies brand, career opportunities, or corporate culture. Freelance Videography: Shoot and edit special events, commercials, documentaries, or training videos.

  • Web Design

    Creating the visual aspects of websites. Creating layouts, designs, and features that display the goods/services in a way that is appealing to the target audience.

  • Motion Graphics Design

    Working across a wide range of media, including film, television, advertising and web content to create artwork and vivid visual effects. Animated Infographics, Title and Credit Sequences, Kinetic Typography, 2D Vector-Based Animation (Explainer Videos) Logos & Websites.
